# Student Career Success Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project explores the factors that drive career success among university students across multiple departments. The goal was to identify patterns between academic performance, field of study, and practical experience.

## Key Insights & "The Why"
During this analysis, I investigated the correlation between **GPA** and the **number of internships**.
* **The Challenge:** High-GPA fields often showed a higher number of internships.
* **The Analytical Question:** Is the GPA driving the success, or do specific fields (like Computer Science or Medicine) have more structured internship pipelines that naturally lead to higher engagement?
* **The Result:** Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize these correlations and help students understand which levers (GPA vs. Experience) matter most in their specific field.

## Tools Used
* **Power BI:** Data visualization and DAX modeling.
* **Analytical Thinking:** Correlation vs. Causation analysis.
